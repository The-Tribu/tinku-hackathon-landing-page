## The first hackathon in Cartago

**TINKU – Ritual CreAItivo** is the first artificial intelligence hackathon in northern Valle del Cauca. For 18 hours, we'll bring together developers, designers, students, and tech professionals in Cartago to create functional prototypes that solve real problems posed by regional companies.

The event will take place on **April 17-18, 2026** at **Universidad Cooperativa de Colombia**.

## What does Tinku mean?

The name **Tinku** comes from an ancestral Andean ritual of encounter between communities. Our hackathon honors that spirit of meeting, bringing together people from different disciplines to create together.

## What can you expect?

- **18 hours** of intensive development
- **5 thematic tracks** to solve real challenges
- **$3.000.000 COP** in main prizes (🥇 $1.500.000 · 🥈 $900.000 · 🥉 $600.000)
- **Technology credits** from Cursor, MiniMax, and Runway
- **Food, coffee and snacks** provided
- **Mentors** to guide you during the event
- **Additional activities**: gaming tournaments, exhibitions and mini-games
